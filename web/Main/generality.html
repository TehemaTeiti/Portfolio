Presentation of my portfolio

As part of my computer training specializing in the Innovative Smart Systems (ISS) at the "Institut National des Sciences Appliquées" in Toulouse, I have written this portfolio to present my experiences linked to this field, but also the knowledge and the skills I acquired through this formation.

This portfolio is divided into four parts:

  • The generality: corresponding to this current page, this is where I introduce my curriculum and give an overview of the ISS training.
  • A descriptive part: all my relevant experiences highlightning the knowledge and skills of this training are presented in this part.
  • A technical part: during my training, I was confronted with problem situations. In this part, I talk about the difficulties I encountered in some situations and how I mobilized my skills and my knowledge, but also resources that I had at my disposal to solve them.
  • An analytical part: finally, in this part, I present all the knowledge and the skills I acquired through this formation.
